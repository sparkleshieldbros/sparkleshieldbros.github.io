The observer who notices patterns and creates bubble shields when feelings get big.

Captain Cash

For Grown-Ups

Stories That Open Real Conversations

Sparkle Shield Bros is built for shared reading, bedtime talks, classroom calm corners, and family repair moments. The superhero layer keeps kids engaged while the emotional skills stay practical.

Calm

Kids practice noticing body clues and slowing down before choosing.

Kindness

Story choices reward helpful words, patience, and care for others.

Courage

Heroes try again, ask for help, and face big feelings with support.

Repair

Families get language for apologies, do-overs, and reconnecting.
