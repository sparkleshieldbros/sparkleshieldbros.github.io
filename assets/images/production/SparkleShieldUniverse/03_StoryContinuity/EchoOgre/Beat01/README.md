# EO-B01 - Opening Image

Book: The Echo Ogre
Spread: 01
Pages: 6-7
Status: prompt
Canonical: approved story board

## Story Text
Maplewood Terrace was where tomorrow grew from kindness.

## Story Purpose
Introduce Maplewood Terrace as warm, wondrous, and alive without overloading the first read.

## Story Function
Opening Image: establish the ordinary world, the emotional promise of the series, and one quiet hint that something purple is watching from the story tree roots.

## Emotional Objective
Wonder, belonging, and curiosity invite the reader into the world.

## World Building
- Feature only three front-and-center systems: Sparkle Grid sidewalk mosaics, the Community story mural/tree, and solar baobab garden life.
- Let other inventions recede or stay off-page so the spread reads as a home, not a tour brochure.

## Page-Turn Hook
Captain Cash races toward a glowing story tree. Something purple watches from inside its roots.

## Illustration Objective
Maplewood Terrace feels joyful, safe, aspirational, and easy to understand before the trouble begins.

## Camera, Lighting, And Color
Camera: High cinematic establishing shot descending toward the brothers' backyard
Lighting: Golden sunrise with warm, breathable neighborhood light
Color: Navy and gold foundation; warm sunrise gold; natural greens; soft cyan technology; no dominant purple except a tiny hidden watcher.

## Prompt Draft
Create a breathtaking full-spread opening image of Maplewood Terrace at golden sunrise, seen from a high cinematic view that descends toward Bubble Boy Kairo and Captain Cash's backyard and the glowing story tree. Keep the first read simple and child-centered by featuring only three memorable world systems: Sparkle Grid sidewalk mosaics glowing under children's footsteps, the Community story mural/tree holding neighborhood memory, and solar baobab garden life collecting warm sunlight. Bubble Boy and Captain Cash should be small enough to show the neighborhood scale but readable enough to feel like the emotional center. Captain Cash races toward the glowing story tree while Kairo follows with curious caution. Hide one tiny purple watcher inside the roots as a page-turn clue. Any floating gardens, drones, living homes, or wind sculptures should be distant background atmosphere only if they do not compete with the main action. Leave calm sky or garden space for one short line of text.

## Production Notes
- Full spread target: 17.25 x 8.75 in / 5175 x 2625 px at 300 DPI unless this is marked as a production-support slot.
- Preserve bleed and gutter-safe areas for InDesign.
- Keep all referenced characters on-model and use Maplewood Terrace continuity for geography.

- Keep the first read emotionally clear; layer franchise details as second-look discoveries.
