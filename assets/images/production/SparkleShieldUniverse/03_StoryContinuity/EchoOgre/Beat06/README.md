# EO-B06 - The Echo Ogre Emerges

Book: The Echo Ogre
Spread: 06
Pages: 16-17
Status: prompt
Canonical: approved story board

## Story Text
Every angry word made the shape bigger.

## Story Purpose
Reveal the Echo Ogre as fractured light made of unresolved emotion, not a monster from outside.

## Story Function
Break Into Adventure: the Echo Ogre emerges as broken stories, shattered geometric patterns, distorted sound waves, floating ancestral symbols, fractured holograms, and corrupted Sparkle Grid light.

## Emotional Objective
Awe and fear arrive together.

## World Building
The Echo Ogre is emotional architecture formed from repeated words, broken stories, distorted murals, ancestral symbols, and Sparkle Grid interference.

## Page-Turn Hook
Then the broken stories stood up.

## Illustration Objective
A new magical problem emerges from the neighborhood's own unresolved feelings.

## Camera, Lighting, And Color
Camera: Low cinematic reveal with children small but brave in the foreground
Lighting: Golden world disrupted by luminous purple fractured light
Color: Navy/gold world base with controlled purple Echo Ogre energy and broken geometric highlights.

## Prompt Draft
Create the first full reveal of the Echo Ogre at the Community Mural and Sparkle Grid junction. The Echo Ogre should emerge as fractured living light: broken neighborhood stories, shattered geometric patterns, distorted sound waves, floating ancestral symbols, mural fragments, and corrupted Sparkle Grid glow forming a giant purple emotional figure. Bubble Boy and Captain Cash stand small in the foreground, stunned but not defeated. Every angry word around them appears to add a fragment to the Ogre. Show Maplewood Terrace beginning to break rhythm around him, but do not make it apocalyptic. Use the Echo Ogre construction, action, and expression references directly; use the environmental guides to make his body feel connected to the world rules.
