# EO-B03 - The Brothers Show Their Gifts

Book: The Echo Ogre
Spread: 03
Pages: 10-11
Status: prompt
Canonical: approved story board

## Story Text
Kairo built worlds in bubbles. Cash made the whole street move.

## Story Purpose
Introduce the brothers' gifts and the family rhythm before conflict erupts, with fewer inventions competing for attention.

## Story Function
Set-Up: show Bubble Boy creating tiny living bubble worlds, Captain Cash energizing one kinetic neighborhood sculpture/path, and the Sparkle Grid responding to both brothers' gifts before their friendly rivalry begins.

## Emotional Objective
Joy shifts toward friendly rivalry.

## World Building
- Keep the spread focused on three systems: Kairo bubble ecosystems, Cash-powered kinetic light/sculpture movement, and the Sparkle Grid reacting underfoot.
- Mom and Dad can be present as warm family support, but they should not introduce a separate invention on this spread.

## Page-Turn Hook
Then the brothers disagreed about whose invention was better.

## Illustration Objective
The brothers are both gifted, joyful, and proud, which makes their coming disagreement understandable without crowding the page.

## Camera, Lighting, And Color
Camera: Bright kinetic ensemble spread with separate readable action zones
Lighting: Late morning sparkle with crisp child-friendly clarity
Color: Bubble Boy orange and bubble blue; Captain Cash green and gold; Mom navy and gold; garden greens; only trace purple in the deep background.

## Prompt Draft
Create a joyful, organized spread showing Bubble Boy Kairo and Captain Cash using their gifts in ordinary Maplewood Terrace family life. Kairo creates tiny living bubble ecosystems with plants, light, and gentle motion safely floating inside. Captain Cash powers one kinetic street sculpture or glowing play path by running in bright green-and-gold trails. The Sparkle Grid should glow most brightly where Kairo's orange bubble light and Cash's green motion trail overlap. Mom and Dad may appear warmly in the background as family support, but do not introduce a separate invention, seed board, or extra technology system. End the composition with the brothers facing each other in playful disagreement, each proud of his own gift. Use the Bubble Boy and Captain Cash action sheets for body language and the Maplewood Terrace environment sheets for continuity.
