# EO-B02 - Strong Heroes Help People Shine

Book: The Echo Ogre
Spread: 02
Pages: 8-9
Status: prompt
Canonical: approved story board

## Story Text
Strong heroes do not just fix machines. They help people shine again.

## Story Purpose
State the emotional philosophy without stopping the story.

## Story Function
Theme Stated: Dad models repair by helping two arguing neighbors reconcile, while the boys witness the principle before they fully understand it.

## Emotional Objective
Calm wisdom and community care become visually magical.

## World Building
- A neighborhood light sculpture relights when people reconcile.
- The Sparkle Grid responds to emotional repair, not only mechanics.

## Page-Turn Hook
The sculpture glows brilliantly while a tiny purple crack appears beneath it.

## Illustration Objective
Dad is repairing a relationship, and the technology brightens because the people repair first.

Keep the existing narrative action: Create a warm, emotionally specific spread near the Neighborhood Green where Dad repairs a neighborhood light sculpture by helping two arguing neighbors reconcile. Bubble Boy and Captain Cash watch from nearby, impressed but still too young to understand the full rule. Make the technology beautiful but secondary to the relationship repair. Add one tiny purple crack beneath the sculpture, almost hidden in the mosaic path, as a visual hook. Use Dad's expression guide for calm leadership and the Maplewood Terrace environment references for exact neighborhood continuity.
