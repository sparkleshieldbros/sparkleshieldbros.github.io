# EO-B02 - Strong Heroes Help People Shine

Book: The Echo Ogre
Spread: 02
Pages: 8-9
Status: prompt
Canonical: approved story board

## Story Text
Strong heroes do not just fix machines. They help people shine again.

## Story Purpose
State the emotional philosophy without stopping the story.

## Story Function
Theme Stated: Dad models repair by helping two arguing neighbors reconcile, while the boys witness the principle before they fully understand it.

## Emotional Objective
Calm wisdom and community care become visually magical.

## World Building
- A neighborhood light sculpture relights when people reconcile.
- The Sparkle Grid responds to emotional repair, not only mechanics.

## Page-Turn Hook
The sculpture glows brilliantly while a tiny purple crack appears beneath it.

## Illustration Objective
Dad is repairing a relationship, and the technology brightens because the people repair first.

## Camera, Lighting, And Color
Camera: Warm medium-wide community scene with Dad at child-eye readability
Lighting: Morning gold reflecting from the repaired light sculpture
Color: Healthy greens and golds with one nearly hidden purple crack under the sculpture.

## Prompt Draft
Create a warm, emotionally specific spread near the Neighborhood Green where Dad repairs a neighborhood light sculpture by helping two arguing neighbors reconcile. Dad should be kneeling or leaning in calmly, open-handed, listening first. The neighbors should visibly soften toward each other as the sculpture relights with gold. Bubble Boy and Captain Cash watch from nearby, impressed but still too young to understand the full rule. Make the technology beautiful but secondary to the relationship repair. Add one tiny purple crack beneath the sculpture, almost hidden in the mosaic path, as a visual hook. Use Dad's expression guide for calm leadership and the Maplewood Terrace environment references for exact neighborhood continuity.
