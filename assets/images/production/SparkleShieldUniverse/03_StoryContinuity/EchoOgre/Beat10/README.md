# EO-B10 - When Kindness Disappeared

Book: The Echo Ogre
Spread: 10
Pages: 24-25
Status: prompt
Canonical: approved story board

## Story Text
The Ogre towered because kindness had disappeared.

## Story Purpose
Create the emotional low point: the Ogre is not stronger because of power, but because repair has vanished.

## Story Function
All Is Lost: the Grid nearly collapses, sky loses color, solar trees stop, gardens wilt, floating homes descend, and Dad walks forward with no gadget.

## Emotional Objective
The story reaches its lowest point and shifts from action to accountability.

## World Building
Solar trees, floating gardens, homes, murals, and the Sparkle Grid all lose function when the community stops repairing relationships.

## Page-Turn Hook
Then Dad walked forward with no gadget at all.

## Illustration Objective
Maplewood Terrace almost goes still because the emotional system is starving.

## Camera, Lighting, And Color
Camera: High overhead 35 mm view with the neighborhood as the main subject and Dad entering without gadgets
Lighting: Color draining from the sky, with only faint gold left around relationships that might repair
Color: Desaturated Maplewood Terrace, wilted greens, dim navy, restrained purple scale; one small warm gold human point around Dad.

## Prompt Draft
Create the All Is Lost spread from a high overhead view of Maplewood Terrace. The Sparkle Grid is nearly collapsed, the sky has lost much of its color, solar baobab trees no longer collect light, gardens wilt, floating homes and garden structures descend safely, and the Echo Ogre towers over the neighborhood. The Ogre is huge not because he is stronger, but because kindness and repair have disappeared. Bubble Boy and Captain Cash should feel small and uncertain. Dad walks forward from the edge of the scene with no gadget, no weapon, no shield, and no glowing technology. His empty hands and calm posture should be the emotional hook. Use the Environmental Deterioration Strip and Environmental State Guide directly, but keep the scene child-safe and emotionally readable.
