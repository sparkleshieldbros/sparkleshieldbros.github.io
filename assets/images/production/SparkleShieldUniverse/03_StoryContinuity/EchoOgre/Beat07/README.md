# EO-B07 - Nothing Works Because Everything Echoes

Book: The Echo Ogre
Spread: 07
Pages: 18-19
Status: prompt
Canonical: approved story board

## Story Text
The harder they pushed, the louder the Ogre became.

## Story Purpose
Show the old superhero strategy failing because the problem is emotional, not physical.

## Story Function
Fun & Games: the brothers use bubbles, light shields, speed trails, decoys, and gravity bubbles. Each attack creates more chaos and grows the Ogre. The hook is Bubble Boy comforting Captain Cash and the Ogre pausing.

## Emotional Objective
Action exhausts itself and opens the door to tenderness.

## World Building
Bubbles, light shields, speed trails, decoys, gravity bubbles, and Sparkle Grid energy are real hero tools, but they fail when used without understanding.

## Page-Turn Hook
Then Kairo put one hand on Cash's shoulder - and the Ogre paused.

## Illustration Objective
Spectacular powers make the problem worse until comfort briefly changes the pattern.

## Camera, Lighting, And Color
Camera: Dynamic action spread with clear cause/effect and a quiet emotional clue at the end
Lighting: High-contrast purple escalation cut with warm gold still visible around the heroes
Color: Controlled spectacle: orange, green, gold, and purple, with the grid darkening as attacks repeat.

## Prompt Draft
Create a kinetic action spread where Bubble Boy and Captain Cash try every familiar hero tool against the Echo Ogre. Bubble Boy launches protective bubbles, light shields, decoys, and gravity bubbles. Captain Cash creates green speed trails and tries quick rescue moves. The Echo Ogre copies everything bigger, louder, and more chaotic. Show the Sparkle Grid darkening or skipping with each forceful attempt. At the far side of the spread or as the emotional focal point, show Bubble Boy pausing to comfort Captain Cash with one hand on his shoulder. That one gentle action causes a small part of the Echo Ogre to soften and pause. The page-turn should come from that unexpected emotional clue, not from a battle victory.
