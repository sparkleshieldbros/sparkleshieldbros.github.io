# EO-B05 - The Neighborhood Acts Wrong

Book: The Echo Ogre
Spread: 05
Pages: 14-15
Status: prompt
Canonical: approved story board

## Story Text
They checked the tech. But the trouble was not in the wires.

## Story Purpose
Make the debate visual: the brothers think the technology is broken, but readers see the neighborhood reacting emotionally.

## Story Function
Debate: tiny systems malfunction. Floating gardens drift lower, murals distort, bird drones lose rhythm, and the brothers misread the problem as technical.

## Emotional Objective
Confusion and concern replace confidence.

## World Building
Floating gardens, animated murals, bird drones, Sparkle Grid lights, and sound sculptures all respond to emotional imbalance.

## Page-Turn Hook
Inside the broken mural, two purple eyes opened.

## Illustration Objective
The brothers investigate the wrong cause while the world gives emotional clues.

## Camera, Lighting, And Color
Camera: Wide neighborhood troubleshooting spread with the boys moving through visible malfunction clues
Lighting: Warm light turning uneven and slightly cool where purple interference appears
Color: Muted greens and golds with small violet distortions, not full darkness.

## Prompt Draft
Create a visually rich debate spread where Bubble Boy and Captain Cash investigate Maplewood Terrace as if the technology is broken. Show floating gardens drifting slightly lower, animated murals distorting, bird drones losing rhythm, Sparkle Grid lights skipping beats, and wind sculptures falling out of tune. The boys should be looking at tools, panels, bubbles, or pathway lights, trying to diagnose a technical problem. Readers should see that the neighborhood is reacting emotionally: purple patterns gather around places where feelings were ignored. In the broken Community Mural, hide a clear pair of purple eyes opening while the boys look away. Keep the tone mysterious but still child-safe and beautiful.
